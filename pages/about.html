About

I am Ravi Sharan, a Free Software enthusiast from Hyderabad. I am currently contracting as Research Assistant at IIT Hyderabad (Yes ! There's an IIT in Hyderabad). My current research interests include a mix and match of Cognitive Radio networks and GSM networks. As part of my everyday job, I spend most of my time reading GSM specs and testing with in-house equipment (baseband stuff!). Work aside, I love playing with my Raspberry Pi and occasionally build Android for my Nexus4.
